Introduction
======
My research vision is to develop efficient AI methodologies that achieve high performance in data-scarce scenarios or without paired data. I'm broadly interested in AI, including CV and NLP, with a focus on improving model performance and addressing challenges like hallucination and consistency through techniques such as reinforcement learning, model architecture, and learning algorithm improvements (e.g., GAN, RAG).

Keywords: Generative Models, Multi-modal Learning, Efficient Learning
